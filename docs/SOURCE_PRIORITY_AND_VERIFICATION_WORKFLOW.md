# Source Priority Hierarchy

1. **Official Election Records**  
   - Primary source, verified official data.

2. **Official Prosecutor Statements**  
   - Statements from official legal entities.

3. **Local/State Journalism**  
   - Reporting from reputable local or state news outlets.

4. **Nonpartisan Datasets**  
   - Data collections created by neutral organizations.

5. **Campaign/Social Media**  
   - Information from campaigns or social media needs validation.

---

# Verification Workflow

To ensure reliability and accuracy in sourcing, please follow this workflow:

1. **Evidence Capture**  
   - Ensure all information is documented with a clear statement of facts.

2. **Quote Attribution**  
   - Attribute quotes to the original speaker or author accurately.

3. **Timestamping**  
   - Include the date and time of the information capture.

4. **Source URL Requirements**  
   - Provide a direct link to the original source of the information.

Adhere to this workflow to maintain the integrity of the data collected.
